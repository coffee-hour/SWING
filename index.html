📖 MMO Academy Guide

🕹️ Core Mechanics & Physics

  • Grapple Hook: Click anywhere or tap Spacebar to shoot a line and swing.
  • Score Scaling: Distance traversed applies your Total Multiplier! Swing backwards to drop into the Underworld.
  • Galaxy Mines: Avoid floating purple mines! If you swing into one, your rope will snap and you will instantly lose 90% of your speed!

⚔️ The Optimized Build

  • Weapons & Coats: Buy in the Black Market using Gold. Weapons add massive Multipliers, while Coats manipulate gravity and hook range.

🛡️ Guild Base & Permissions

  • R1 Recruit: Welcome to the guild! (Cannot chat yet).
  • R2 Member: Unlocks Guild Chat access.
  • R3 Officer: Can approve new Join Requests.
  • R4 Co-Leader: Can spend War Coins, edit the Notice Board, and Kick lower ranks.
  • R5 Leader: Complete control. Can promote or demote any member.

🐉 Alliance Boss Raid

  • World Boss Raid: Every score registers damage against the massive Abyssal Leviathan. Earn War Coins when it dies!
