📖 MMO Academy Guide

🕹️ Core Mechanics & Physics

  • Grapple Hook: Click anywhere or tap Spacebar to shoot a line and swing.
  • Score Scaling: Distance traversed applies your Total Multiplier! Swing backwards to go to the Underworld.

⚔️ The Optimized Build

  • Weapons & Coats: Buy in the Black Market using Gold. Weapons add massive Multipliers, while Coats manipulate gravity and hook range.

🛡️ Guild Base

  • Guild Leader Tools: Leaders can spend War Coins on massive Title Buffs, select Crest Emojis, and set custom hex Colors (or Secret Codes)!

🐉 Alliance Boss Raid

  • World Boss Raid: Every score registers damage against the massive Abyssal Leviathan. Earn War Coins when it dies!
