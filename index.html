Released: June 7, 2026

Released: June 7, 2026

  • Alliance Chat: Live Alliance Chat added (requires R2 Member status to talk).

Released: June 6, 2026

  • Fair War Coins: Defeating the Boss now distributes War Coins dynamically to ALL participating Alliances based on their exact damage percentage!

v1.5.0 - The Hazards Update

Released: June 5, 2026

  • Galaxy Mines: Floating purple hazards added to the sky. Hitting one snaps your rope and drops momentum by 90%.

Released: June 4, 2026

  • Live Operations: The server can now push live global events (like the 3x Boss Frenzy) directly to all connected clients!

Released: June 3, 2026

  • Exponential Pets: Guild Pet exchange rates now actively double every 10,000 tokens to balance late-game scaling.

Released: June 2, 2026

  • Guilds: Create/join Alliances. Added active Titles and custom Crest buffs.
